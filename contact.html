TEDx was created in the spirit of TED's mission, "ideas worth spreading," supporting independent organizers who want to create a TED-like event in their own community. TEDxUTD expands on this mission, bringing ideas worth spreading to UT Dallas.

The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events, including ours, are self-organized.
