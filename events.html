Viewing & Discussions

TEDxUTD has a strong tradition of hosting monthly Viewing & Discussion events, or V&Ds, where we showcase a theme and view TEDtalks matching that theme, followed by an intriguing, friendy group discussion exploring different viewpoints on a range of topics.

Past V&Ds have been held about Women, Education, and Empowerment, Our Food and Our Health, and more.

TEDxUTDSalon

Salon events are recurring events that keep the community engaged between conferences. They're a unique gathering that let's the conversation continue in person. Our Salon events feature live speakers, notably professors from within the UTD community with big ideas on the world at large.

Follow us @tedxutd to stay up to date with all things TEDxUTD



TEDx was created in the spirit of TED's mission, "ideas worth spreading," supporting independent organizers who want to create a TED-like event in their own community. TEDxUTD expands on this mission, bringing ideas worth spreading to UT Dallas.

The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events, including ours, are self-organized.
