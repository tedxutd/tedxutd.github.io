Sunday, April 13th, 2014

Challenges & Initiatives

Each of us face challenges every day that we tackle alone, or with our friends and family. However, there are bigger challenges out there — challenges like shortages of food, or our impact on the environnment — that we have to all tackle together. We're bringing those challenges to light, along with the initiatives to help tackle them.

TEDxUTD2014 Speakers

ALBERTOK

How I Flipped My Perspective on Life, Literally

A talk on overcoming fears one’s through the use martial arts, with a sample performance of his trademark flip, his personal metaphor for this activity.

VAIBHAVPRAKASH

Using RFID to Make Buildings Smarter and More Environmentally Friendly

A talk on using RFIDs & other tracking technology to gain insights on the environment and save energy, touching on a range of topics inclufing cybersecurity, big data, and sustainability.

CAITLYNNFORTNER

Feeding the Future with MAS & GMOs

A biotech-focused talk on using Genetically Modified Foods and Market Assisted Selection to solve food shortages & make food better.

DR.SHAWNCARRAHER

Using Strategic Goal Setting to Differentiate Yourself

A business-oriented talk on using principles of organizational behavior, international business, and operations management to help make your goals clearer using an effective seven-principle strategy.

DR.PAULFISHWICK

Finding Computing in Everyday Objects

A talk about the ubiquity of computing in even the most mundane things we take for granted, as well as the visualization of computing into works of art.
